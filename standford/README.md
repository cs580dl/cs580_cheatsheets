# Cheatsheets from AI/ML courses at Stanford University

## Goal
These cheatsheets aim at summing up in the same place all the important notions that are covered in Stanford's for AI/ML courses, and include:
- Convolutional neural networks (CNN), recurrent neural networks (RNN), as well as the tips and tricks to have in mind when training a deep learning model.
- All elements of the above combined in an **ultimate compilation of concepts**, to have with you at all times!

## Translation

These cheatsheets are available in several languages from the original authors at [https://github.com/afshinea](https://github.com/afshinea). 

Would you like to see these cheatsheets in your native language? You can help us translating them on [this dedicated repo](https://github.com/shervinea/cheatsheet-translation)!
